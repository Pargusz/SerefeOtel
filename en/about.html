The Story of Şerefe

Harmony of Elegance and Stone

Şerefe Hotel Alaçatı combines the charming atmosphere of the Aegean with a deep-rooted understanding of hospitality. Located in the heart of Alaçatı, our hotel offers an unforgettable experience by blending the most elegant examples of traditional stone craftsmanship with the comfort of modern luxury.

With our courtyard where you will find peace in every detail, our turquoise pool where you can relieve the fatigue of the day, and our carefully designed rooms, we see this place not just as a hotel, but as your home in Alaçatı.

We transform the celebration and health wishing theme carried by the name "Şerefe" (Cheers) into a lifestyle in every guest's stay.
